Greek Salad Recipe

Description

Greek salad is a refreshing and healthy dish made with fresh vegetables, olives, and feta cheese. The salad typically includes lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, bell peppers, and kalamata olives, dressed with a simple vinaigrette of olive oil, lemon juice, and herbs.

Greek salad is a traditional dish in Greece, dating back to ancient times.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of lettuce, chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/2 cup crumbled fetacheese
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine chopped lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and bell pepper.
  2. Add kalamata olives and crumbled feta cheese on top of the vegetables.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together extra-virgin olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to combine.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy.
