Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian main dish consisting of alternating layers of long, flat pasta noodles, tomato or cream sauce, and cheese (typically, mozzarella, parmesan, or ricotta, or a combination of all three).

Lasagna is one of the oldest pasta dishes in Italy, originating in Naples in the Middle Ages.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • Tomato paste
  • 1 egg
  • Chopped Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the Noodles for 8 min.
  2. Add the tomato paste.
  3. Add the egg and the Parsley.
